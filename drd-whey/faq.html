PRODUCT

Q What's the difference between whey isolate and concentrate?

Whey isolate is more refined, containing 90%+ protein with minimal lactose and fat. Concentrate typically has 70-80% protein with more lactose. We use isolate because it's cleaner, easier to digest, and delivers more protein per scoop.

Q Is DRD Whey suitable for lactose intolerant people?

Most likely yes. Whey isolate contains minimal lactose (less than 1%), which is typically well-tolerated even by those with lactose sensitivity. However, if you have severe lactose intolerance, consult your doctor.

Q When should I take DRD Whey?

Within 30 minutes post-workout is ideal for muscle recovery. You can also use it as a meal replacement or snack to hit your daily protein targets. There's no wrong time—just consistent use.

Q How many servings per container?

Each tub contains 30 servings (30 scoops). At one scoop per day, that's a month's supply. Most customers order 2-3 tubs to save on shipping.
