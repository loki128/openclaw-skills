# Cleopatra Delights - Bakery Project

**Owner:** Mom (engineer, African woman)
**Location:** Jacksonville
**Experience:** 3 continents (Asia, Africa, North America)
**Specialty:** International desserts from around the world
**Background:** Cooked for whole families for years

## Brand Story Elements
- Engineer turned baker (precision + passion)
- 3-continent journey (cultural fusion)
- Family-centered (cooked for whole families)
- Global desserts (authentic recipes from travels)
- African heritage + international influence

## Key Messaging
- "Engineered for delight"
- "3 continents, 1 kitchen"
- "Family recipes from around the world"
- "Precision meets passion"

## Signature Products
- **Brownies** — Fudgy, dense, engineered to perfection
- **Cookies** — Crispy edges, chewy centers, global flavors
- **Cakes** — Layered, moist, family-size celebrations

## Product Lines by Origin

### Egyptian-Inspired
- **Basbousa brownies** — Semolina-brownie fusion with rose syrup
- **Date-filled cookies** — Maamoul-style shortbread
- **Umm Ali cake** — Egyptian bread pudding as layered cake

### Greek-Inspired
- **Baklava brownies** — Walnut-honey brownie with phyllo crunch
- **Kourabiedes cookies** — Almond shortbread with powdered sugar
- **Galaktoboureko cake** — Custard-filled semolina cake

### Turkish-Inspired
- **Turkish delight brownies** — Pistachio-rose water fudge brownies
- **Tahini cookies** — Sesame-forward crisp cookies
- **Revani cake** — Citrus-soaked semolina sponge

### North American Classics (Engineered)
- **Triple-chunk brownies** — Precise chocolate distribution
- **Chewy chocolate chip cookies** — Tested for optimal texture
- **Layered celebration cakes** — Feed the whole family
